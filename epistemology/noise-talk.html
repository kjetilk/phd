    The Copernican Revolution

    You probably heard the story, which Kuhn popularized, how astronomy became increasingly complex in the Middle Ages, finally to be overthrown by a better heliosentric system. This is a myth.

    In 1975, Owen Gingerich computed where the planets actually were, where the old models predicted them to be, and where contemporaries predicted them to be. He showed that contemporaries used an unchanged Ptolemaic model, and that the magnitude of the errors were about the same.

    Theory

    What is a theory?

    Theory
    A theory is a coherent set of hypotheses that are held as true after having been subjected to vigorous testing by a scientific community.
    • Can we formulate Theory of Semantic Web? Something to test against
    • Philosophers aren't terribly useful...
    • Roughly: Hypotheses should be easy-come, easy-go things
    • Theories are collections of hypotheses
    • Coherentism in its pure form is OK for stuff like string theory, or formal frameworks. Many points of attack, can bring down entire theory.
    • No connection to real world, bad. No acknowledgement of community, bad

    Theory of the Semantic Web
